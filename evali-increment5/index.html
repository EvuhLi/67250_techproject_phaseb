Welcome to MonoMuse

MonoMuse is a contemporary museum dedicated to exploring the intersection of art, technology, and human creativity. Through immersive exhibitions, interactive installations, and rotating collections, MonoMuse invites visitors to discover how digital innovation is transforming the way we create, experience, and understand art.

Whether you are a first-time visitor or a returning member, our exhibitions offer new perspectives on the role of technology in culture and everyday life. Plan your visit to experience cutting-edge exhibitions, hands-on experiences, and events designed to inspire curiosity and creativity. MonoMuse also hosts workshops, artist talks, and community events throughout the year, making it a hub for learning and engagement. Join us and become part of a growing community that celebrates the fusion of art and innovation.
