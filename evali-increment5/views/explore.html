Our Mission: MonoMuse is dedicated to bridging the worlds of art and technology. We believe that creativity thrives at the intersection of innovation and human expression, and we strive to make that experience accessible to everyone.

Who We Serve: MonoMuse welcomes artists, students, families, educators, and curious minds of all ages. Whether you are a seasoned art lover or stepping into a museum for the first time, there is something here for you.

Our Story: Founded in 2020 in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, MonoMuse began as a small pop-up gallery showcasing local digital artists. What started as a passion project quickly grew into a full-scale museum experience, attracting visitors from across the country.

Milestones

  • 2020 - MonoMuse founded as a pop-up gallery in Pittsburgh
  • 2021 - Opened our first permanent exhibition space
  • 2022 - Expanded to include interactive and immersive installations
  • 2023 - Welcomed our 100,000th visitor
  • 2024 - Launched the MonoMuse Student Arts Program
