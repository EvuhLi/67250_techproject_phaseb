Current Exhibitions

Digital Reflections

Step into a world where mirrors, light, and code collide. Digital Reflections is an immersive installation that uses motion-tracking technology to transform your movements into living art. Every visitor becomes part of the exhibit, creating a unique visual experience that is never the same twice.

Pixels and Paint

Explore the evolution of visual art from canvas to screen. Pixels and Paint showcases side-by-side works from traditional painters and digital artists, revealing surprising connections between brushstrokes and binary code. This exhibition celebrates how the tools change but the human desire to create remains the same.
